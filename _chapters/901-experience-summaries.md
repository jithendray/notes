### Senior Data Engineer @ NeenOpal

#### 1. AWS WAR and proposal finalizations

**Resume point:** Improved AWS partnership by conducting over 10 AWS Well-Architected reviews identifying and remediating key architectural gaps across performance, cost optimization, and security pillars, driving a 30% increase in client retention. Architected data-driven solutions and led proposal finalization for key projects.

**Brief description:**
What?
- In my role, I conducted over 10 AWS Well architected reviews for various clients across different industries, focusing mainly on their ETL workloads but also some application hosting workloads. 
- These efforts not only helped mitigate risks but also led to stronger AWS partnership for my company, resulting in increased client retention and opportunities from new customers.
- I also led proposal finalization and requirement gathering calls for some key projects.

How?
- I used AWS Well-Architected Tool, which leverages the AWS Well architected framework - to assess risks in all six pillars - security, cost optimization, performance efficiency, operational excellence, reliability, and sustainability. 
- By collaborating with clients, I identified areas of improvements, such as implementing credential rotation for RDS/Redshift as a security measure and setting up AWS cost budgets and alerts for better cost management.
